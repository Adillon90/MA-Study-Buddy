### L071 | 1.0 | Easy
A Massachusetts producer must report a change of address to the Commissioner within:
- A) 10 days
- B) 15 days
- C) 30 days
- D) 60 days
**Answer: C** — Address changes must be reported within 30 days.
*Citation: M.G.L. c. 175 § 162M(f)*

### L072 | 1.0 | Medium
A lapsed Massachusetts producer license may be reinstated without retaking the exam within:
- A) 6 months of the renewal date
- B) 12 months of the renewal date
- C) 24 months of the renewal date
- D) 3 years of the renewal date
**Answer: B** — Reinstatement without re-examination is allowed within 12 months; the penalty is double the unpaid renewal fee.
*Citation: M.G.L. c. 175 § 162M(c)*

### L073 | 1.0 | Easy
Offering a prospect something of value not stated in the policy as an inducement to buy is:
- A) Twisting
- B) Rebating
- C) Coercion
- D) Defamation
**Answer: B** — Rebating is prohibited, and both giving and receiving a rebate violate the law.
*Citation: M.G.L. c. 175 §§ 182–184*

### L074 | 1.0 | Medium
An insurer must file a notice of producer appointment within how many days of executing the agency contract or receiving the first application?
- A) 5 days
- B) 15 days
- C) 30 days
- D) 45 days
**Answer: B** — The appointment notice is filed within 15 days, whichever event comes first.
*Citation: M.G.L. c. 175 § 162S*

### L075 | 1.0 | Hard
A producer is convicted of an administrative violation in another state. They must report it to the MA Commissioner within:
- A) 10 days of the charge
- B) 30 days of final disposition
- C) 60 days of final disposition
- D) 90 days of the hearing
**Answer: B** — Administrative actions in another jurisdiction must be reported within 30 days of final disposition.
*Citation: M.G.L. c. 175 § 162V*

### L076 | 1.0 | Easy
A producer may NOT use the existence of the Massachusetts Life and Health Insurance Guaranty Association:
- A) When filing a claim
- B) As an inducement to purchase insurance
- C) In a complaint to the Division
- D) When reinstating a policy
**Answer: B** — The guaranty association gag rule bars using its existence as a sales tool.
*Citation: M.G.L. c. 175 § 146B*

### L077 | 1.0 | Medium
Massachusetts continuing education after the first renewal requires how many hours per 36-month period, including 3 hours of Ethics?
- A) 24 hours
- B) 30 hours
- C) 45 hours
- D) 60 hours
**Answer: C** — Subsequent renewals require 45 hours per 36-month period, including 3 hours of Ethics.
*Citation: M.G.L. c. 175 § 162M*

### L078 | 1.0 | Hard
An insurer terminating a producer for cause must notify the Commissioner within 30 days, and then notify the producer within:
- A) 5 days
- B) 10 days
- C) 15 days
- D) 30 days
**Answer: C** — After notifying the Commissioner within 30 days, the insurer must notify the producer within 15 days, by certified mail for a for-cause termination.
*Citation: M.G.L. c. 175 § 162T*
