This is the source for each state's CE line in its tutor, and a quick reference for "what's the CE in X?" questions.**

## The shortcut
**Default = 24 hours / 2 years / 3 ethics.** Sixteen of the nineteen states follow it exactly. Only three break the pattern: **Massachusetts** (45 hours / 36 months), **New York** (~15 hours single line), and **Virginia** (16 hours single license / 24 multi). A few add per-line minimums (CT 6, SC 8, VA 8) or use a birth-month cycle (GA, ME, SC).

## How this is used in the app
- Each state's tutor states its own CE in the regulation section (MA and CT live).
- A compact `CE_BY_STATE` reference is appended to every tutor prompt so any tutor can answer "what's the CE requirement in [state]?" if asked.
- When a new state launches, copy its row here into that state's regulation content.
