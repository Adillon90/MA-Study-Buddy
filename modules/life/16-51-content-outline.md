## Full topic outline (for module mapping)
- **3.0 Basics (20%):** insurable interest; personal uses (survivor protection, estate creation, cash accumulation); business uses (buy-sell, key person, executive bonus, COLI); determining amount (human life value vs needs); classes of policies (group/individual, permanent/term, ordinary/industrial, par/nonpar, fixed/variable); premiums (mortality/interest/expense, net single/gross annual, modes); producer responsibilities (guaranty law, policy summary, buyer's guide, replacement, field underwriting, app procedures/backdating, delivery); individual underwriting (app, producer report, APS, inspection report, MIB, medical/HIV, risk classification).
- **4.0 Policies (15%):** term (level/ART/level-premium/decreasing); whole life (straight/limited-pay/single/graded/modified/interest-sensitive/equity-index); flexible premium (adjustable/universal/indexed/variable); specialized (joint first-to-die, survivorship second-to-die, juvenile); group life; credit life.
- **5.0 Provisions/Riders (24%):** standard provisions (ownership, assignability, entire contract, free look, grace, reinstatement, incontestability, misstatement of age, exclusions, interest on proceeds); beneficiaries (designations, succession, facility of payment, revocable/irrevocable, common disaster, spendthrift); settlement options (cash, interest only, fixed-period, fixed-amount, life income single/joint); nonforfeiture options; policy loans/withdrawals; dividend options; disability riders (waiver of premium/cost, disability income, payor); living benefits (accelerated, LTC riders); riders covering additional insureds; riders affecting death benefit (ADB, guaranteed insurability, COLA, return of premium).
- **6.0 Annuities (14%):** principles (accumulation vs annuity period; owner/annuitant/beneficiary); immediate vs deferred (SPIA, deferred); payment options (life contingency, annuities certain); products (fixed, equity-indexed, market-value-adjusted, variable); uses (LTC riders, qualified plans, IRAs, education).
- **7.0 Federal Tax (7%):** taxation of personal life insurance; MECs (7-pay test); non-qualified annuities (exclusion ratio); IRAs (traditional/Roth); rollovers/transfers; 1035 exchanges.
- **8.0 Qualified Plans (6%):** general requirements; federal tax treatment; plan types (SEP, Keogh/HR-10, profit-sharing/401(k), SIMPLE, 403(b) TSA).
